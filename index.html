Hello.

Amin Al Hazwani is a Designer at Mozilla. Based in Berlin, he’s currently working on Firefox’s Photon Design System.

He’s one of the organizers of the Berlin Design Systems Coalition meetup.

Former Professor in Web and Media Design at the Free University of Bolzano, Italy, he taught at the MA in Eco-Social Design. He also wrote for A List Apart.

From 2009–16 he ran a one-man studio from Italy, specialized in design for screen. Clients included Google, Accurat, Unibz, Studio Mut, and more.
