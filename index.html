Amin Al Hazwani

I lead DesignOps at Accurat, a data-driven research, design and innovation agency in Milan, Italy.

Previously, I lived in Berlin and worked for Mozilla. I designed browser experiences for TVs and smart assistants powered by Amazon’s Alexa. I also launched the Firefox Design System to support the not-for-profit multi-platform browser factory.

I taught Web and Media Design at the M.A. in Eco-Social Design in Bolzano, Italy. During this tenure I co-wrote a piece for A List Apart.

Between 2010–16 I ran a one-man studio specialized in design for screens. I produced work for Google, Studio Mut, Arillo, Unibz and many more.
